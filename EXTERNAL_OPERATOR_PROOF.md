# External Operator Proof

## Objective

Prove that actions performed outside the direct control of Veridian remain attributable and auditable.

## External Action Chain

External Operator
        ↓
Identity Verification
        ↓
Action Attribution
        ↓
Evidence Collection
        ↓
State Verification
        ↓
Receipt Generation
        ↓
Accountability

## Verification Rule

Unknown Operator:
FAIL CLOSED

Missing Identity:
FAIL CLOSED

Missing Attribution:
FAIL CLOSED

Missing Evidence:
FAIL CLOSED

## Proof Requirement

Veridian must prove:

- who performed the action
- when the action occurred
- what action was performed
- what evidence exists
- what state changed

## Accountability Rule

Every external action must be linked to:

- identity
- evidence
- receipt
- state transition

## Guarantee

No external action may exist without attributable ownership and supporting evidence.
